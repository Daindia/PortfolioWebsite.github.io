Loan Approval Bias Assessment

This project analyzed a loan approval dataset to evaluate if there's potential bias in loan approvals based on the self-employment and education status. I performed exploratory data analysis in MySQL to calculate approval rates while controlling for CIBIL score and income in order to prevent false evidence of bias.

Key Findings:

  • Graduates: 62.45% approval rate
  • Non-graduates: 61.98% approval rate
  • Self-employed: 62.23% approval rate
  • Non-self-employed: 62.20% approval rate

The difference in percentage points between corresponding groups is not large or significant enough to indicate meaningful bias. The analysis concluded that loan approval outcomes are determined primarily by creditworthiness, with education and employment status playing no significant role.
